## UI/UX Pro Max Skill
- **Search Command:** `npx uipro-cli search "<query>" --domain <domain>` or `python3 .gemini/skills/ui-ux-pro-max/scripts/search.py "<query>"`
- **Available Domains:** `product`, `style`, `typography`, `color`, `landing`, `chart`, `ux`
- **Supported Stacks:** `html-tailwind`, `react`, `nextjs`, `astro`, `vue`, `nuxtjs`, `nuxt-ui`, `svelte`, `swiftui`, `react-native`, `flutter`, `shadcn`, `jetpack-compose`
- **Usage:** Analyze user requests for UI/UX elements, use the skill to fetch recommended styles, colors, and fonts, and apply them.
